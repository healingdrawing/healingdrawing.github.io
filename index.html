Wheel Creator 2018


Create a parametric 3D model of car wheel.
Result can be saved/loaded uses txt file, exported into OBJ file, that later importing into other 3D tools.

Available presets:
-few types of bolts;
-few type of grips;
-configured form of metal wheel part;
-configured form of wheel tire;
-configured colors of wheel elements;
-configured three lights(ambient, directional, point);
-render scene into PNG file;
-wireframe model displaying;
-can be saved as html file and used without internet connection.
