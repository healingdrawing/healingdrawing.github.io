AI prime
AI prime place(the place of your team partner before enemy serve will executed), controlled automatically, but deviation can be controlled using "PARTNER" menu.



From left to right variants means:
  • similar as PLAYER along X axis, and parallel along Y axis, with some restrictions. Also with this scheme AI will try switch user on blocking, after serve will executed, as possible, while AI have enough skill power.
  • similar as PLAYER along X axis, and mirrored along Y axis. Also with this scheme AI will try switch user on blocking, after serve will executed, as possible, while AI have enough skill power.
  • placement close to serve area, and close to center of court along Y axis.
  • placement close to side line along Y axis, far from serve area.
  • placement close to court corner, balanced.
Defense position of AI character, when enemy execute attack, will be controlled automatically, depends on enemy team toss direction.

When enemy team toss directed close to center court area along Y axis, defender will move close to center too, to try cover maximum area for weak attack or forward attack.

When enemy team toss directed close to side line of court area along Y axis, defender will move diagonally in most of cases, to try cover maximum area of attack.
