AI serve
AI serve place, and serve target, can be controlled using next variants from scheme:
Green 2 is your team partner AI, controlled using scheme above. Blue enemy team defenders, in prime position.

From up to down, the right area variants means:
  • serve to court corner, near to enemy player.
  • serve to enemy player.
  • serve between enemy team characters.
  • serve to enemy partner.
  • serve to court corner, near to enemy partner.
From up to down, the left area variants means:
  • serve from place along Y between court corner and enemy player.
  • serve from place along Y as enemy player.
  • serve from place along Y between enemy team characters.
  • serve from place along Y as enemy partner.
  • serve from place along Y between court corner and enemy partner.
AI serve type can be controlled using percentage slider, under scheme:
from 0% - serve always from ground, to 100% - serve always in jump.

AI serve style can be controlled using percentage slider, under scheme:
from 0% - serve always slow(soft in jump, or high from ground), to 100% - serve always fast(strong in jump, or normal from ground).
