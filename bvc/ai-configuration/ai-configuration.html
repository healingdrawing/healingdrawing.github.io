AI configuration
  • your partner AI settings placed inside "PARTNER" menu.
  • AI settings will saved only after "MENU" button pressed inside "PARTNER" menu.
  • your player(character, controlled by your actions) AI settings placed inside "PLAYER → AI" menu.
    You can control your player AI manually using settings gui, in case of you want control your player behavior, when your team order will be placed on server, that other players can play match versus bots configured from your team AI both, or your partner AI from gui settings, and your player AI from data collected from your actions.
    Manually controled player AI functionality was added because AI core generator from user actions demonstrated not very good behavior, and sometimes looks weird.
  • to control AI behavior section, check control behavior for this section
  • choose the master index, it will get the value first. Other will devide proportionally % which master does not use.
    F.e. if master is 50% , and other is 50% 50% 50% 50%, then master variant of behavior will get from random behavior generator 50% and other variants is 12.5% each.
