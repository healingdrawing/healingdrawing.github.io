User toss
To control toss (upper soft touch the ball) enough click on court active area.

To execute toss near to net click on enemy team side area.

To execute toss far from net click no your team side area.

Toss target place along screen Y axis depends on your click Y position.

Toss deviation depends on distance from ball to your character hands in touch moment.

When character have not enough time for move to comfort toss position, the fish(ground falling) defense can be executed and toss target position will not so neat as planned. In this case if toss was targeted close to net, there is chance toss will be retargeted to enemy side.

When your toss will too far from your partner position, your partner still will try to attack, but can miss or fail, and this will your fault.
