User blocking
Character blocking position determined automatically, but additional simulation of hands deviation in ball contact moment can be controlled by click on enemy team side or your team side.

Visually there is no difference in animation of blocking, but internally result of ball contact hands checking, can be different, depending of click.

To execute blocking forward, without deviation, click on enemy side, close to court center along Y axis.

To execute blocking with displacement along Y axis, click on enemy side, close to court corner, to prefered direction of blocking.

To execute blocking with displacement both hands each in opposite direction, how it used in real game sometimes, when blocking executed close to net center, click on your team side, close to court center along Y axis.

To move back in defense, and not execute blocking, click on your team side, close to court corner, to prefered defense move direction.

Click should be executed, after enemy team character execute toss, and your team character come to blocking position.
